## 🔷 What makes this different

This is not a life simulator where you grind for achievements. It's a relationship survival game where your wife's ring (🛟) and your husband's ring are the core health bars — and they're inversely linked. Every purchase you make drains him; every rubella (💋) he earns feeds you. The moment either ring gets too thin, something breaks.

The game simulates an entire life cycle: you marry, the husband goes to the office to earn coins, you spend them on dresses, chocolates, and furniture. You get pregnant, have children who orbit around you like moons, grow them up, and watch them leave. Then your husband dies or you divorce, and you find a new one — possibly different, possibly the same face with a different career level.

What makes it genuinely unique is the **ring mechanic** — a visual, real-time representation of financial and emotional strain in a marriage. There's no menu, no inventory screen (until you open the house), no quest log. Everything happens on a single canvas with emoji characters, floating text, and physics-based item collisions. The husband bounces off items like a pinball. The judge appears when bills go unpaid and you have to kiss-battle him. Cats multiply and penalize your husband's income. The "other women" (сучки) spawn and fire compliments at your husband that you have to intercept.

It's a darkly comedic take on the pressures of marriage, family, and financial responsibility — all rendered in emoji and canvas.

## ✨ Features

- 💍 **Ring-based marriage system** — Your ring and your husband's ring are health bars that drain and refill based on spending and earning. If either gets too thin, the marriage breaks
- 👩‍❤️‍👨 **Procedural husband generation** — Each husband has a random emoji face, skin tone, age, and career level. Previous husbands are tracked and avoided
- ♑️ **Capricorn archetype** — 35% chance your husband gets a zodiac trait with an ice blade that attacks enemies and a boss blade at career level 10+
- 💼 **Career progression** — 22 humorous job titles from "Наблюдатель тапочек" to "Скрывающийся за Замами", with office visits unlocking furniture and abilities
- 👶 **Full family simulation** — Get pregnant, give birth, raise children who orbit around you. They grow from babies to teenagers to graduates, and eventually leave home
- 🏠 **House decoration system** — Open the house screen, browse a furniture catalog, and drag-and-drop items onto a 9x9 grid. Each piece has a price and affects your budget
- 🎰 **Casino & alcohol mechanics** — Unlock slot machines and alcohol items. The husband can get drunk, casinos can drain or boost your coins
- ⚖️ **Judge boss battles** — Unpaid bills summon a Judge who fights you. You defeat him by kissing (Space) while dodging his bullets
- 🐱 **Cat taming system** — Cats spawn over time, you can tame them and they orbit your wife. Too many cats penalize your husband's ring
- 👗 **Contraception mechanic** — Dresses act as protection. Without them, kisses lead to pregnancy
- 🏦 **Budget management** — Separate budgets for husband and wife, displayed in the house screen. Track every purchase and salary
- 📊 **Session statistics & rating** — Track marriages, children, departed kids, career levels, and death ages across all playthroughs
- 🎂 **Age progression & remarriage** — Years pass on the remarriage screen with pressure messages from family and friends that change based on your age
- 🌍 **Chunk-based biome world** — The game world is divided into chunks with different biomes (green, pink, blue, brown, etc.)
- 📱 **Responsive canvas** — Full-screen canvas rendering with emoji sprites, floating text, and spring physics on items
- 🔔 **Music toggle** — Background music can be toggled on/off from any screen
- 📖 **Built-in tutorial** — Step-by-step tutorial for first-time players explaining the ring mechanics and basic controls
