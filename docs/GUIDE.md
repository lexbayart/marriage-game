# My Best Wife — Complete User Guide

> A complete guide for "My Best Wife v9.0.4" — a single-file canvas game about marriage, family, and financial survival.

---

## 🖱️ Navigation / Getting Started

1. Open `index.html` in any modern browser
2. You'll see the main menu with "My best wife 👸" title
3. Click **💍 Выйти замуж 👩‍❤️‍💋‍👨** to start a new game
4. Or click **🤦‍♀️ Обучение девиц 🩰** to read the tutorial first

**Main menu buttons:**
- **💍 Выйти замуж** — Start a new marriage/game
- **🤦‍♀️ Обучение девиц** — Tutorial screen with game rules
- **🏆 За глаза** — Rating/statistics screen showing all past sessions
- **🧘‍♀️ Настройки** — Settings screen
- **🔞 Выйти из игры** — Close the game

## 🎮 How to Play

### The Ring System (Core Mechanic)

Every marriage has two rings:
- **🛟 Your ring (wife)** — Grows when you spend money, shrinks when your husband earns
- **🛟 Husband's ring** — Grows when he earns money, shrinks when you spend

**The golden rule:** Keep both rings balanced. If either ring gets too thin (small radius), the marriage is in danger.

### Basic Gameplay

1. **Your wife (👸)** moves around the canvas with **WASD** keys
2. **Your husband (👨)** moves autonomously, collecting coins (🪙) and office items
3. Press **Space** to kiss your husband (💋) — this directs him toward you but can lead to pregnancy
4. Use **dresses (👗)** as contraception to control when to have children

### Items on the Map

The world spawns various items your husband can collect:
- **🪙 Coins** — Husband's income. He collects them and brings them to you
- **💼 Office** — Husband goes to work, earns money, may unlock career progression
- **🛋️ Couch** — Husband rests here, recovering
- **👗 Dresses** — Contraception. Husband picks them up to "protect" you
- **🍫 Chocolate** — Gift for your wife, increases her ring
- **💄 Lipstick** — Beauty purchase, increases wife's ring
- **💍 Ring** — Special item, increases wife's ring
- **🍺 Alcohol** — Unlocks after anger blade activates 4 times. Husband can get drunk
- **🎰 Casino** — Slot machines. Unlocks after collecting 10 alcohol items
- **📋 Bills (receipts)** — Fly at you. If husband doesn't pay them, the Judge appears

### Pregnancy & Children

1. Kiss husband without a dress → pregnancy chance
2. After pregnancy, a baby (👶) appears and orbits around your wife
3. Babies grow into children (👦/👧), then teenagers (🧑), then adults
4. Children orbit around you like moons
5. When children turn 18 or graduate, they leave home
6. Graduated children are tracked in the rating screen

**Feeding children:** Open the house screen (H) and interact with children to feed them (increases their ring size).

**Reading to children:** Books (📚) can be collected by children. A child who collects 10+ books before age 18 becomes a "wunderkind" (вундеркинд).

## 🏠 House System

Press **H** during gameplay to open the house screen.

### House Grid
- 9x9 grid where you place furniture
- Each cell can hold one furniture item
- Walls and doors are pre-placed

### Furniture Catalog
- Left sidebar shows available furniture to buy
- Each item has a price and emoji icon
- Click an item to buy and place it on the grid
- Drag furniture to reposition

### Budget Display
- **🚹 Husband's budget** — How much the husband has earned/spent
- **🚺 Wife's budget** — How much the wife has spent

### House Dirt
- Smoking (🚬) creates dirt in house cells
- Dirt is tracked by row,col coordinates
- Checked annually

### Husband's Desire Cells
- Special cells in the house grid that show what the husband wants
- Pulsing border indicates desire

## ⚔️ Combat & Enemies

### The Judge (Судья)

Appears when you accumulate unpaid bills (billsDebt). The judge:
1. Approaches your husband
2. Locks onto him
3. Shoots bullets at him
4. You must kiss (Space) to counter-attack

**How to defeat the Judge:**
- Keep kissing your husband while he's near the judge
- Dodge the judge's bullets
- The judge has a fixed text message and will eventually retreat

### Other Women (Сучки)

Female characters that spawn and fire compliments at your husband:
- They fly across the screen
- Their compliments (💋) home in on your husband
- You need to intercept them by positioning your wife between them and your husband

### Cats (Кот)

- A cat (🐱) spawns after 60 seconds without conception
- You can tame the cat by interacting with it
- Tamed cats orbit your wife
- Multiple cats (up to 3) can orbit
- Too many cats penalize your husband's ring
- Cats age and can die (default death age: 15 years)

### Capricorn Ice Blade (♑️)

If your husband has the Capricorn archetype (35% chance):
- An ice blade rotates around him
- It attacks enemies (judges, sluts)
- Each dirt (unpaid bill) adds a rotation
- The blade is active for 2 seconds per rotation

### Boss Blade (♑️)

At career level 10+ ("Начальник"), the Capricorn husband gets a positive green blade that gives +5 bonus.

## 🎰 Casino & Alcohol

### Alcohol
- Unlocks after the anger blade activates 4 times
- Alcohol items (🍺) spawn on the map
- Husband can pick them up and get drunk
- Drunk state affects husband's behavior and quotes

### Casino
- Unlocks after husband picks up 10 alcohol items
- Casino slot machines (🎰) spawn on the map
- Husband can play slots and win/lose coins
- The slot machine has a final icon determined at the start of the slow-down phase

## 🔄 Remarriage System

When your husband dies or you divorce:

1. The remarriage screen appears with a carousel of 5 potential husbands
2. Years pass automatically (faster as you age)
3. Each year, the carousel advances with new potential husbands
4. Eventually, one husband will "match" (pulse with golden glow)
5. Click **💍 Выйти замуж** to accept, or **🚫 Я достойна лучшего** to skip

### Age Pressure Messages

Different age groups receive different messages from family and friends:
- **18-25:** Optimistic, supportive messages
- **26-35:** Subtle hints and questions
- **36-45:** Pressure and worry
- **46+:** Despair and disappointment

When you receive a proposal, messages turn positive and encouraging.

### State Carryover

When you remarry:
- Your wife ages 1-3 years
- Children from previous marriages carry over
- Tamed cats carry over (and age)
- Unlocked items (lipstick, ring, offices) persist
- Pregnant wife state carries over
- Career level is tracked per husband

## ⚠️ Known Limitations

- Single HTML file — no cloud save, progress is lost if you clear browser data
- No mobile touch controls — designed for keyboard/mouse
- Emoji rendering depends on OS/font support — some emojis may look different across platforms
- No multiplayer — single player only
- Audio uses Web Audio API generated sounds — no actual music files
- The game world is infinite (chunk-based) but items and events follow fixed patterns
- Children's "off-screen aging" may produce unexpected results with complex family states
- The judge battle can be extremely difficult if bills debt is very high
- Cat population can grow unbounded if not managed, heavily penalizing the husband
